# Working Agreements (Luís & João) – 42 Minishell (v1)

## Branching
- `main` is the stable branch.
- Work happens on short-lived branches: `feature/`, `fix/`, `docs/`, `chore/` with Jira key in name.

## PRs and reviews
- All merges to `main` happen via PR.
- Once branch protection is enabled: **2 approvals required** (Luís + João).
- Use the PR template and include how to test.

## Mandatory vs Bonus
- Mandatory first.
- Bonus stays parked until mandatory is stable and complete.

## Daily sync
- 10 minutes: what moved, what’s next, what’s blocked.
- Update Jira after the sync.
